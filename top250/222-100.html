Single 12oz bottle ($9.99 OH) poured into tulip snifter.

A: Pours black with a thin brown head. Some lacing.

S: Strong brandy smell gives way to molasses, dark chocolate, and apple, of course.

T: Again, lots of brandy giving way to all the flavors described above, plus subdued barrel notes. There is a little heat but it should mellow with time. Definitely best quaffed at around 55 degrees or warmer.

M: Full bodied, smooth and creamy.

O: Likely to taste even better with a few months on it, this beer drinking mighty fine fresh. Dessert in a glass but not at all cloying.
