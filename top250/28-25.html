Notes & Commercial Description:
Beer added by: American_Hawk on 04-24-2015

Brewed with local, raw, wildflower honey. Single-hopped with Mosaic with our friends from Artaic.

Artaic Double IPA was brewed with 100% Mosaic hops, including two massive dry hop additions and local raw wildflower honey. Juicy candied peach and nectarine up front, backed up with a little white wine on the nose that continues straight through in the flavor. Pillowy soft mouthfeel, a restrained bitterness on the finish. Artaic is an innovative Fort Point business that creates incredible mosaics with a robot (illustrated on the bottle label!). Our new Artaic barrel room mosaic is visible when you drop in to the brewery's retail shop...come check it out!
