Reviewed from notes taken during tasting.

Golden amber clear body, head is fizzy leaving not much lacing. Big kiwi and strawberry aromas, fresh berries and tart acetic take center stage. Taste is similar with oak also present. Tart fruits, berries, fresh tropical fruit dominate the tongue. Nearly perfectly balanced. Very tasty.

Was very impressed with this. Would love to see what age does to this.
