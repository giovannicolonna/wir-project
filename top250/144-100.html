Tasted 4/25/14:

The most recent I've had from Fiddlehead. Comes out darker than a lot of DIPAs, a dark yellow verging on orange with a small white head. Throws off LOTS of juicy, citrusy hop aromas on the aroma, with a slight letdown on the taste. Not that there's not a ton of hop character on the palate -- because there is -- but I think this one smells hoppier and more bitter than it actually is. Slightly too sweet for my taste, as well. In fairness, I was gifted this growler so I can't comment as to the freshness, and going on the carbonation is may have been sitting in the fridge for a few days.

Overall, a great DIPA. I don't think Fiddlehead is quite up to par with the big names in Vermont DIPAs yet, but if they keep improving the way they have over the past couple of years they could be before long.

OGFGabv, Jul 10, 2015
4.84/5  rDev +9.8%
look: 4.75 | smell: 5 | taste: 4.75 | feel: 5 | overall: 4.75

Served from the tap at Prohibition Pig in Waterbury, VT. To put it simply, this is definitely a top echelon IIPA that deserves to be ranked with the the likes of Double Sunshine and Heady Topper.

A - Pours a dark orange hue with a medium, off-white head. Darker than many DIPA's, especially the local VT competition. Very hazy, definitely an unfiltered beer.

S - Spectacular aromas of citrus, tropical fruits, and pine. Malt smells are very subdued. A spectacular example of what a great DIPA should smell like.

T - Just as the nose would imply, lots of citrus upfront, with hints of other tropical fruits and a resinous pine flavor that lingers on the pallet after your swallow. Like the other big vermont-style DIPA's, this beer is very well balanced and not just another hop bomb. The malts aren't too sweet and compliment the hops well.

M - Medium body, tight carbonation, leaves a somewhat juicy feel on the mouth.

O - Definitely a top echelon beer! This deserves to be ranked up there with the other great Vermont DIPA's. I make trips to VT every few months to grab Hill Farmstead, Lawson's, and Heady Topper. Fiddlehead just earned themselves a spot on the rotation.
