Poured cold into a wine glass; consumed on the night of the release.
Look = 4.25 Pours a clear golden yellow with 1 finger of off white head. Active carbonation following the pour fades as consumed. The head dissipates but a decent lace remains. Good look for a Gueuze-style beer.
Aroma = 4.5 An acidic sourness dominates the aroma but is highlighted nicely by light citrus and barnyard funk notes. Complex but great.
Taste = 4.5 Strong sourness as soon as it hits the tongue, which lasts throughout but fades enough on the backend to allow the other flavors to come through. Tangy, citrus notes and some mild barnyard on the finish.
Feel = 4.75 Medium body with high carbonation; fizzy on the tongue, a substantial feel to this one, fits the style perfectly.
Drinkability = 4.25 Terrifically sour but complex and will keep you drinking.
Overall = 4.475 A terrific world class Gueuze-style American Wild Ale here by New Glarus.
