Poured into a Funky Buddha Brewery snifter. No freshness date.

A- Pours a dark amber color with a 1/2 inch off-white head that retains well before becoming a paper-thin ring around the edge of the glass and a half sheet of sheer surface foam. Resilient lacing cascades down the glass.

S- Smells like pineapple juice but with a sweet malt backing.

T- Taste is of not-so-tart grapefruit juice mixed with sweet wort. I like the tropical fruit essence (hence the name) that combines with the beery malt presence. Super drinkable with a mild bitterness.

M- Semi-tangy with medium carbonation and a body leaning toward the lighter side.

O- Very solid next-gen IPA (just coined the term). Has that juicy, fruity character while still maintaining that medium-abv drinkability. A new standard of "go-to" IPA.
