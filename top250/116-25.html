Appearance - The beer poured a golden copper color with a bright white head.

Smell - The nose might be best described as candied grass.

Taste - My first impressions is spicy grass. Other notes include orange, tea, and biscuity malts. All of the notes going on here work well together. There is a slightly warmish aftertaste with notes of orange and spice.

Mouthfeel - There is a smooth clinginess to the mouthfeel. The spice really sticks around.

Overall - Triple IPA is not an official style. That said, this is not the most heavy imperial IPA. It is delicious.

monkeybeerbelly, Aug 07, 2015
4.49/5  rDev 0%
look: 4.25 | smell: 4.5 | taste: 4.5 | feel: 4.5 | overall: 4.5

Look: Dark orange body with a white foamy head that easily slips from the sides of the tulip glass it has been poured into. The body is lighter than Hop Showers, solid, and even colored orange compared to some of their other IPAs.
Smell: Candied fruit and citrus aroma up front. Some watered down orange juice smell. Sharp hop smells linger. I could almost smell some rubbing alcohol. That would actually be interesting. I have not drank rubbing alcohol since I was in alcohol rehab in the military.
Taste: BOOM! BAM! SMACK! This has a good balanced taste, with gentle sweetness, that does not overwhelm your taste buds. Where's the alcohol?
Feel: Thick and heavy in the chomper.

Overall - Over the past few days, from Other Half, I have had Galaxy IPA, Nelson, Green Diamonds, and Hop Showers. I am glad I saved the best for last. This one is a hit with me. This is high octane...well, it should be if it is supposed to be a triple. This is a beer to seek out. A contender in the IPA bracket.

Triple IPAs to me have always been a crapshoot in a way. I've had a lot of good ones(never had PtY), but one has never blown me away until AGE. This is an amazing beer in every way. It's high ABV, but you don't taste it. It pours less cloudy than any other OH beer I've had. The nose is very strong- pine, grass, dank. The taste is incredible, a feast for the palate. All of the intense flavors from the nose combine to provide one of the best IPA experiences I've ever had. Could not recommend higher.

A side note- the first can I had I poured into a glass and it was fantastic. Second can I drank directly from the can and it was a revelation.
